## Overview
- Sign panels and vinyl mounted boards available in multiple materials for interior or exterior use.
- Design team tailors artwork, sizing, and finishes to match business branding.
- Production lead time is typically 5–10 working days; expedited requests handled on enquiry.

## Ordering Workflow
1. **Free Quote** – choose a standard panel size or request a complimentary site visit for measurements and photos (or supply your own).
2. **Design Stage** – send existing artwork for proofing, or provide logos/briefs for the studio to develop visuals; in-house logo creation available.
3. **Approval** – review mockups and quote; once signed off the job is scheduled for production.
4. **Fulfilment** – collect finished boards from Kidderminster or arrange delivery (from £10).

## Material Options

### Aluminium Composite (Durable Exterior Board)
- Construction: twin coated aluminium skins with solid polyethylene core.
- Size: up to 8 × 4 ft (2440 × 1220 mm); larger signs use multiple panels side by side.
- Thickness: 3 mm with 0.15 mm aluminium skin.
- Finish: supplied white matt for vinyl wrap or decals; printable surface accepts gloss, matt, anti-graffiti, or dry-wipe laminates.
- Fixings: drillable for screw installation; colour-matched screwcaps supplied with installation jobs (available separately for supply only). Panels can arrive pre-drilled.

### Perspex Acrylic (Luxury Interior or Exterior)
- Use cases: premium building signage or wall art where a high-end finish is required.
- Sizes: custom clear or 35 opaque colours; thickness options 3, 5, 8, or 10 mm; square or rounded corners.
- Stock formats (5 mm clear): round 100/150/300 mm, square 100 × 100 mm, rectangles up to 420 × 297 mm, ovals from 150 × 75 mm to 300 × 200 mm.
- Finishes: gloss opaques (Clear, Opal, White, Black, Dark Grey, Grey, Violet, Sky, Azure, Brilliant Blue, Navy, Lime, Green, Kelly Green, Brown, Burgundy, Hot Pink, Red, Tomato, Orange, Golden Yellow, Yellow, Ivory, Cream, Gold, Silver, Mirror Silver, Mirror Gold); pastel matt or mid-gloss (Grape, Salmon, Peach, Lemon, Mint, Baby Blue, Powder Blue, Lavender). Mirror acrylic not warranted outdoors; only Clear and Opal are translucent.
- Fixings: typically installed with metal stand-offs (19×19, 13×19, or 25×25 mm) in chrome or satin; panels can be pre-drilled with recommended stand-off counts.

### Aluminium Sign Tray (Robust Exterior Sign)
- Material: 3 mm aluminium composite folded into a pan tray; multiple colours and finishes.
- Maximum single-piece sizes (includes returns): standard colours 3000 × 1460 mm, special finishes 2400 × 1200 mm. Larger trays supplied as multiple panels with tongue-and-groove joining strips.
- Colours: matt or gloss White, Black, Anthracite, Grey, Cream, Yellow, Orange, Red, Burgundy, Brown, Green, Dark Green, Azure, Deep Blue, Mirror Silver/Gold, Brushed Silver/Gold/Copper/Black.
- Fixings: delivered with required hardware for wall mounting.

### Foamex (Lightweight Interior Board)
- Applications: exhibitions, indoor signage, short-term exterior messaging.
- Sizes: up to 8 × 4 ft; common paper sizes available.
- Thickness: 3, 5, 10, or 19 mm.
- Finish: white surface ready for vinyl wrap or printed graphics; compatible with gloss, matt, anti-graffiti, or dry-wipe laminates.
- Fixings: mount using double-sided tape, Velcro, cable ties, or screws for added durability.

### Correx (Lightweight Temporary Exterior Board)
- Material: waterproof corrugated plastic ideal for temporary outdoor signage.
- Maximum size: 1230 × 1000 mm; stock sizes include 24×18 in (610 × 457 mm) up to 48×39 in (1230 × 1000 mm).
- Colours: White, Black, Yellow, Red, Green, Blue (vinyl wrapping/custom decals available).
- Fixings: hang with cable ties externally or tape/Velcro indoors thanks to low weight.

## Customisation & Finishing
- Printed vinyl graphics handle complex art, gradients, photographs, metallics, fluorescent, glitter, or holographic effects.
- Cut vinyl delivers crisp lettering/logos without a background and excels for solid colours.
- Lamination upgrades improve durability (e.g., anti-graffiti or dry-wipe for menu boards).
- Design support covers everything from layout tweaks to full creative direction or logo development.

## Installation & Support
- Panels built to specification using premium materials and high manufacturing standards.
- Professional installation (including drilling, screwcaps, access equipment) available across the Midlands; self-install kits include pre-drilled holes and fixings on request.
- Contact the team for guidance on selecting materials, finishes, customisation techniques, installation approach, or accelerated timelines.

## Care Tips
- Wipe surfaces with mild soapy water and non-abrasive cloths.
- Inspect exterior hardware periodically, especially multi-panel joins or high-wind sites.
