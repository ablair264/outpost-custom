## Overview
- Free-standing signs (A-frames, swing boards, chalkboards, forecourt signs) capture passing footfall.
- Frames supplied in multiple styles, colours, and weather ratings; existing frames can be refurbished with new graphics.
- Typical production takes 5–10 working days; rush jobs evaluated individually.

## Ordering Workflow
1. **Free Quote** – choose from standard frames or bring your own for rebranding.
2. **Design Stage** – submit artwork for proofing or brief the design team for new visuals/logos.
3. **Approval** – sign off mockups and pricing to enter production.
4. **Fulfilment** – collect locally or arrange delivery (from £10).

## Product Highlight: Eco Swing Pavement Sign
- **Best For:** windy locations thanks to a water-fillable base with wheels.
- **Frame:** steel tubing in black or white; double-sided aluminium panel swaps out easily.
- **Base:** recycled black PVC with integrated handle; fill with water for extra weight.
- **Panel Sizes:** Small 430 × 625 mm, Medium 500 × 750 mm, Large 588 × 917 mm.
- **Weights:** Small 11 kg, Medium 16 kg, Large 13 kg empty / 31 kg water-filled.
- **Includes:** guy ropes, stakes (where applicable), optional add-on panels with windows/doors.

## Customisation Options
- **Printed Vinyl:** ideal for multicolour artwork, gradients, textures, photos, metallic, fluorescent, glitter, or holographic effects; best for detailed campaigns.
- **Cut Vinyl:** crisp lettering/logos without background, perfect for solid colours or simple graphics.
- **Chalkboard Inserts:** optional for promotions that change daily.
- Consultancy available to recommend the right technique for brand goals and environmental exposure.

## Build Quality
- All frames manufactured from high-quality materials, built to withstand exterior conditions (specify wind exposure to match frame type).
- Replacement panels or graphics available so frames can be reused across campaigns.

## Support
- Contact the team for help selecting frame type, size, customisation method, design assistance, or installation guidance.
